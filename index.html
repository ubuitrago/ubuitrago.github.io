About Me

I grew up in San Antonio, Texas as the son of first-generation immigrants— my Dad in the Air Force, my Mom a hospice aide—where I learned early that hard work makes the "American Dream" possible. A STEM camp in high school introduced me to programming and set me on the path to engineering. I earned my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from UT Austin in 2020, but quickly realized my passion was in tech: it moved faster, was more accessible, and anyone could build the next killer app with open-source tools. Today, I focus on software engineering, security, and DevOps—building things that make a difference.
